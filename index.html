Trending Dishes:

Chow Mein

Chinese dish of stir-fried noodles, veggies, & protein (chicken, beef, shrimp, tofu). Crispy or soft, savory & versatile. Popular worldwide.

Chow Mein - Rs.500

-Chow mein noodles (fresh or dried)
-Cooking oil (vegetable, canola, or peanut oil)
-Garlic (minced or chopped)
-Ginger (minced or chopped)
-Onion (sliced)
-Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, carrots, cabbage, and bean sprouts)
-Protein of your choice (chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu) - optional
-Soy sauce
-Oyster sauce
-Salt and pepper -Chicken or vegetable broth (for added flavor and moisture)
Chicken Manchurian

Indo-Chinese dish. Fried chicken in tangy sauce with garlic, ginger, soy. Served as appetizer or with rice.

Chicken Manchurian - Rs.650

-Cooked rice (preferably cold, as it fries better)
-Cooking oil (vegetable, canola, or sesame oil)
-Mixed vegetables (such as carrots, peas, corn, and diced bell peppers)
-Eggs (beaten)
-Soy sauce
-Salt and pepper
-Green onions (chopped) for garnish
-Boneless chicken pieces (cut into bite-sized chunks)
-All-purpose flour (for coating)
-Cornstarch (for batter and sauce thickening)
-Eggs (for batter)
-Ginger and garlic paste
-Soy sauce
-Tomato ketchup
-Red chili sauce (adjust according to your spice preference)
-Vinegar
-Sugar
-Salt and pepper
-Water or chicken broth
-Cooking oil (for frying and sautéing)
-Green onions (chopped) for garnish
Dumplings

Bite-sized pockets of dough filled with meat, veggies, or sweets. Steamed, boiled, or fried, across diverse cuisines like Asian and European.

Dumplings - Rs.480

-Ground meat (pork, chicken, beef, or a combination)
-Napa cabbage or Chinese cabbage (finely chopped)
-Green onions (chopped)
-Ginger (minced)
-Garlic (minced)
-Soy sauce
-Sesame oil
-Salt and pepper
-Optional: Shrimp, mushrooms, or other vegetables for added flavor and texture
-Dumpling wrappers (can be bought pre-made or made from scratch using flour and water)
-Water (for sealing the dumplings)
-Cooking oil (for frying or pan-frying) OR
-Chicken or vegetable broth (for boiling)
Dipping Sauce:
-Soy sauce
-Rice vinegar
-Sesame oil
-Optional: Minced garlic, minced ginger, and chili flakes for added flavor
